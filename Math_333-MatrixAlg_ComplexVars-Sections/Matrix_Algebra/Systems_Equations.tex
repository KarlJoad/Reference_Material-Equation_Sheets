\subsection{Systems of Equations}\label{subsec:Systems_Equations}
A \nameref{def:Matrix} can be used to represent a system of equations.

For example,
\begin{align*}
  x + 2y + 3z &= 5 \\
  2x - y + z &= 3 \\
  x - y + 5z &= 2
\end{align*}
can be represented with a set of matrices as follows
\begin{equation*}
  \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 2 & 3 \\
    2 & -1 & 1 \\
    1 & -1 & 5 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
    x \\
    y \\
    z \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  =
  \begin{bmatrix}
    5 \\
    3 \\
    2 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
